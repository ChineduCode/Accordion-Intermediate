Frequently Asked Question

What is the muscular system ?
The muscular system controls numerous functions, which is possible with the signficant differentiation of muscle tissue morphology and ability. The muscle tissue is responsible for functions such as mantenance of posture, locomotion, and controls of various circulatory systems.
Involuntary muscle?
Its the muscle thats not controlled by individual volition. Its also a muscle movement not under conscious control (e.g. the beating of the heart)
Voluntary muscle?
The muscle that is normally controlled by individual volition. Or muscle movement under conscious control (e.g. deciding to move the forearm and leg)
